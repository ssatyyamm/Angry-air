🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING MUSIC ISSUES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

❌ File won't play?
   ✅ Must be named exactly: perfect.mp3
   ✅ Check file extension (not .m4a, .wav, etc.)
   ✅ Try converting to MP3
   ✅ Ensure it's in the same folder as HTML

❌ Music too loud/quiet?
   ✅ Edit script.js, find: music.volume = 0.3
   ✅ Change to 0.1 (quieter) or 0.5 (louder)
   ✅ Range is 0.0 to 1.0

❌ Autoplay blocked?
   ✅ This is normal browser behavior
   ✅ Just click anywhere on the page
   ✅ Music will start automatically after click
